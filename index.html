✦ Our Story

Fashion Born
in Bogura

Nagar Style started with a simple belief: every man in Bangladesh deserves access to premium, stylish clothing at honest prices. Founded in Bogura Sadar, we are a homegrown brand with a big city vision.

We source only quality fabrics and work with skilled craftsmen to bring you pieces that look great, feel comfortable, and last long — whether it's a casual polo for a summer day or a fine Punjabi for a special occasion.

Our name, Nagar Style, reflects our roots — "Nagar" meaning city in Bangla, and "Style" being the promise we make to you.

👔
🌿
Quality First
We never compromise on material quality. Every fabric is selected for durability, comfort, and style — especially important in Bangladesh's warm, humid climate.
💰
Honest Pricing
Premium fashion shouldn't break the bank. We keep our prices fair and transparent so that great style is accessible to everyone in Bogura and beyond.
🤝
Community Rooted
We are proud to be a Bogura business. We support local suppliers and aim to be a fashion name that the city is proud of — built by the community, for the community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? ▼

Browse our Products page, select your item, choose your size, and click "Add to Cart". Once you're ready, go to your Cart and click "Proceed to Checkout". You can also contact us directly via WhatsApp to place an order.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept bKash, Nagad, and Cash on Delivery (COD). For bKash/Nagad payments, please send to our merchant number and share the transaction ID via WhatsApp.

Do you deliver outside Bogura? ▼

Yes! We deliver all over Bangladesh via courier service. Delivery outside Bogura typically takes 2–3 business days. Delivery charges apply based on your location.

What is your return/exchange policy? ▼

We offer a 7-day return or exchange policy. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Contact us via WhatsApp to initiate a return.

How do I know which size to order? ▼

Each product has a size chart available. As a general guide: S (chest 36"), M (chest 38"), L (chest 40"), XL (chest 42"), XXL (chest 44"). If unsure, feel free to WhatsApp us and we'll help you pick the right size.

Is Cash on Delivery available? ▼

Yes! Cash on Delivery is available for all locations within Bogura district. For outside Bogura, COD is available subject to courier availability. Please confirm with us via WhatsApp before placing a COD order from another district.

How can I track my order? ▼

Once your order is dispatched, we'll send you the tracking number via WhatsApp or SMS. You can use it to track your parcel on the courier's website.
